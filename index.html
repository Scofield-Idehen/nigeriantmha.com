    To create a safe space for teenagers to thrive and become better equipped for the world

    Our Plan

    About Us

    Creating mental health awareness one teen at a time, by creating events and activities centered around inspiration, growth and wellbeing of teenagers.

    Our Mission

    To provide education and opportunities for teenagers to identify early signs of mental health issues by organizing programs and creating an enabling environment for teenagers to share experiences and get the needed support.

    Our Plan

    To educate teenagers on the necessary skill to help develop more confident and sound teens that will be able to stand out and be shining lights to others to follow.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to provide a safe space for teenagers by promoting the social-emotional development of teenagers.
